Story

Red Dead Redemption 2 is an epic tale set in the late 1800s, in the twilight of the Wild West. The game follows the story of Arthur Morgan, a trusted member of the Van der Linde gang.

The Van der Linde gang, led by the charismatic Dutch van der Linde, is on the run from the law. The gang's way of life is threatened as they face pressure from rival gangs, law enforcement, and advancing modern civilization.

As Arthur, you navigate a vast open world, engaging in a variety of missions and activities. Along the way, you interact with a diverse cast of characters, form deep bonds with your fellow gang members, and confront moral choices that shape the destiny of the gang and Arthur himself.

The story explores themes of loyalty, redemption, and the pursuit of freedom in a changing world. Experience the highs and lows of life on the frontier as you ride through breathtaking landscapes and face the harsh realities of survival.
